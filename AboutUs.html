About Cathy's Creative Cakes

My name is Willow O'Rourke and the name 'Cathy's Creative Cakes' is decidated to my mother Cathy O'Sullivan for teaching me passionately and skillfully her masterery of baking and sugarcrafting skills. My mother was a well-known wedding cake specialist all of her life, dutifully creating masterpiece wedding cakes for brides, far and wide. Ten years ago, I decided to set up Cathy's Creative Cakes providing excellence in Wedding Cake making and design nationwide. We have celebrated much success over the years with the business growing from strength to strength. My Mother's ethos is carried throughout the business in everything we do, "excellence is achieved through love of what you do and listening to whay is asked for". As a company, we continously source ingredients and expertise locally, as much as possible. We are a small team who are decicated to providing excellence and masterery is everything we do to in deliverying special creations for every wedding.

Thanking all of you who support us

Willow O'Rourke - CEO and Owner
