Welcome to Cathy's Cake Creations

Based in Newbridge, Co. Kildare, Cathy's Creative Cakes is an award-winning, luxury wedding cake design studio founded by Willow O'Rourke. Renowned for deliciously different flavour combinations, our bespoke wedding cakes exude modern elegance with natural handmade sugarflowers being a signature style feature. We pride ourselves in having cakes that combine taste and style in equal measure and are honoured that our cakes have been featured in many prestigous publications in Ireland and the United Kingdom.
